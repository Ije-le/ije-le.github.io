Road traffic crashes: Dangerous states to drive in Nigeria

ICIR · 2023

Nigeria’s road traffic crash report shows the country has one of the highest rates of road accidents and fatalities, with the Federal Capital Territory and several states like Nasarawa, Kaduna, Ogun, Oyo, and Lagos consistently recording high numbers of crashes and deaths largely due to speed violations, poor compliance with traffic laws, and other risky driving behaviours.
